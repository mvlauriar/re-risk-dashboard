Methodology & Data Sources

Composite Risk Score Methodology

Our proprietary risk assessment model combines four critical components that most analysts examine in isolation:

Affordability Risk (25% weight)

  • Price-to-income ratios compared to historical norms (40%)
  • Mortgage payment as percentage of median income (40%)
  • Rental parity analysis (20%)

Climate Risk (25% weight)

  • Catastrophic event probability (30%)
  • Insurance premium trajectory (40%)
  • Infrastructure vulnerability assessment (30%)

Market Liquidity Risk (25% weight)

  • Days-on-market trend analysis (30%)
  • Inventory absorption rates (30%)
  • Price reduction frequency (40%)

Cost Trajectories Risk (25% weight)

  • Property tax assessment trends (50%)
  • Insurance premium growth (50%)

Reality Gap™ Index Methodology

Our proprietary Reality Gap™ Index measures the disconnect between seller expectations and market reality through:

  • List price to final sale price ratio (40%)
  • Percentage of listings with price reductions (40%)
  • Time-to-price-reduction velocity (20%)

Data Sources

Our analysis incorporates data from multiple authoritative sources:

  • Housing transaction data: MLS listings across 100 metro areas
  • Climate risk assessments: FEMA, NOAA, and proprietary climate modeling
  • Economic indicators: Bureau of Labor Statistics, Federal Reserve
  • Property tax data: County assessor offices in analyzed jurisdictions
  • Insurance premium data: Aggregate data from major carriers

Ranking & Categorization System

Each metric is normalized on a 0-100 scale with the following risk categories:

  • Critical Risk (80-100): Severe market vulnerability with high probability of correction
  • High Risk (60-79): Significant imbalances indicating potential market stress
  • Moderate Risk (40-59): Some concerning factors offset by stabilizing elements
  • Low Risk (20-39): Generally stable conditions with manageable risk factors
  • Minimal Risk (0-19): Highly favorable market conditions with limited downside

This methodology is updated quarterly to incorporate emerging data and refine predictive accuracy. Subscribers receive notification when significant methodology improvements are implemented.
